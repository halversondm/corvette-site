The Corvette Page



Welcome to the Corvette Page! This site is designed to give those that do not have an understanding behind the fascination of the Corvette for the past 47 years.  The Corvette has epitomized the American racing scene for this time and for those who do not understand why take a look at some of the pictures on the Corvette Pictures page.  How was the Corvette developed over the past 47 years?  Check out the A Corvette History page to learn about the innovations and the developments that came from concept to production.  The Corvette has always been at the top of the new products list with GM.  For example, in the 1960s the Corvette was the first car to receive 4-wheel disc brakes while other cars had 4-wheel drum brakes.  In the 1980s, the Corvette was one of the first to receive fuel-injectors for each cylinder.  Also a first in the 1980s was the introduction of Anti-Lock Disc Brakes.
